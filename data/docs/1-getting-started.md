# Getting Started with DriftDraft

DriftDraft helps you create a **strategic website plan or landing page plan** from a **marketing perspective**. It streamlines the planning process and makes it easier to communicate your ideas to a web developer, designer, or marketer.  

You can also **find competitors**, **check domain availability**, and download your generated plan as a **PDF** for free.

## **Managing Your Credits**
DriftDraft operates on a **credit-based system**:  
- **AI Hint Generation:** **1 credit per AI hint.**  
- **Plan Generation:** **4 credits per full plan.**  
- **Check Available Credits:** Go to the **Account Page**.  

Credits can be obtained via:  
- **Subscriptions** (monthly credit allowance).  
- **One-time top-ups** (purchased credits never expire).
