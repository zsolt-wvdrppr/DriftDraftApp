# Agent Panel Guide

## What is the Agent Panel?
The **Agent Panel** allows you to manage and collaborate with other users by reviewing their AI-generated website plans. As an agent, you can invite users to connect with you and view their project summaries.

---

## How to Become an Agent

### 1. Set Your Agent Name
- Choose a **unique agent name** that will be used for referrals.
- Your agent name helps users find and connect with you.
- Once set, this name will be used for inviting and managing users.

### 2. Invite Users to Connect
- After becoming an agent, you can send invitations to users.
- Once they accept, you will gain access to their AI-generated website plans.

### 3. View and Manage Referrals
- Once a user accepts your invitation, their project details will appear in your **Agent Panel**.
- You can **view their AI-generated website plans**, but you cannot edit them.

---

## What Can Agents See?
- **AI-Generated Plans**: View detailed website structures and recommendations created by the AI.
- **Project Summaries**: See key insights to help guide your referrals in their website development.
- **User Sessions**: Track the AI-generated sessions of your connected users.

---

## Important Notes
✅ You can only have **one active agent name** at a time.  
✅ Users can **change their agent name**, but it requires confirmation.  
✅ You can **remove a connected user** if needed, and users can also disconnect from you.  
✅ **Agent names must be unique** and at least **5 characters long**.
