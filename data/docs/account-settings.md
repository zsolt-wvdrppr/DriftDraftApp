# Account Settings Guide

## Accessing Account Settings
You can access your **Account Settings** by clicking on your **display name** at the top of the page, next to the user icon.

---

## Managing Your Account

### **Changing Your Display Name**
- You can change your display name anytime in the **Account Settings**.

### **Changing Your Email Address**
- To change your email address, you will need to **contact support** at:  
  ✉️ **support@wavedropper.com**.

---

## **Managing Your Agent Connection**
If you have an **agent** or **pending agent requests**, you can:
- View your **active agent's email address**.
- **Revoke your agent** at any time, preventing them from viewing your AI-generated plans.
- If revoked, the agent will **no longer see your plans** until they reinvite you and you accept.
- If you have **multiple pending agent invitations**, they will be displayed in a dropdown.
- Clicking on an agent request will **set that agent** as your active referral.

**Note:**  
- If you receive an invite link and you **already have an agent**, the invite will not override your current agent.
- If you **have no agent set**, you can accept a new agent invitation directly.

---

## **Becoming an Agent**
### **What Does It Mean to Be an Agent?**
As an **Agent**, you can:
- **Invite users** to connect with you.
- **View their AI-generated plans** in the **Agent Panel**.
- If a user has started the questionnaire but **not yet generated a plan**, you will see it listed but **can only view the plan once completed**.
- **Download plans as PDF** for reference or further use.

### **Eligibility Requirements to Become an Agent**
To be eligible, you must have:
- A **payment method** added to your account.
- An **active subscription** OR **at least 10 top-up credits** in your balance.

### **Setting & Changing Your Referral Name**
- As an agent, you need to **set a unique referral name**.
- Your referral name will be **included in your invite links**.
- You can **change your referral name**, but:
  - **Only the latest referral name will work** for invitations.
  - **Older invite links with previous names will not work**.

---

## **Sending Credits to Invitees**
- To send credits, the **invitee must first sign up**.
- **Only top-up credits** can be transferred to invitees.
- You cannot transfer **promo credits** or **subscription-based allowance credits**.

---

## **Managing Agent Connections**
- By default, when a user **clicks on an invite link**, they will be prompted to **confirm the invitation**.
- If they **accept**, they will be **connected with their agent**.
- This connection **can be revoked at any time** in the **Account Settings**.

---

## **Agent Mode & Its Purpose**
- **Agent Mode** is designed for **individuals or businesses** who **create websites or landing pages for clients**.
- Agents can view and manage their **invitees' AI-generated plans**.
- For more details, check the **Agent Panel** section in the documentation.

---

2. **Become an agent** and start inviting users.
3. **Send credits to your invitees** to support their projects.
4. **Read more about the Agent Panel** in the documentation.
